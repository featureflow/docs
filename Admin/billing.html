Admin Billing

The Billing tab allows you to manage your subscription, view your current plan details, and monitor your usage.

Billing Information​

At the top of the page, you'll see your billing information including:

  • Payment method (e.g., card ending in XXXX)
  • Billing email
  • Account holder name
  • Phone number

Current Plan and Usage​

The billing page displays your current plan along with your plan limits and current usage.

Limits shows what's included in your plan:

  • Seats — number of team members allowed
  • Audit Log — whether audit logging is enabled
  • A/B Testing — whether experimentation features are available
  • Monthly Unique Users — maximum unique users per month
  • Monthly Impressions — maximum feature flag evaluations per month
  • Teams — whether team management is enabled

Usage shows your current consumption against these limits with visual progress bars.

Managing Your Subscription​

Manage Billing Details​

Click Manage Billing Details to access the billing portal where you can:

  • Update your payment method
  • Change billing and shipping information
  • View your invoice history

Update Subscription​

Click Update Subscription to change your plan. You can choose between monthly or yearly billing and select from available plans.

Available Plans​

Featureflow offers several plans to suit your needs:

  • Free — Single seat, 1 project, 2 environments. Great for trying out Featureflow.
  • Standard — $25/seat/month. Unlimited flags, projects and environments with 5,000 monthly active users.
  • Pro — $45/seat/month. Everything in Standard plus 10,000+ monthly active users, REST API, and Audit Log.
  • Enterprise — Custom pricing. High retention audit logs, millions of MAU, experimentation, and enterprise support.
