# Slack Integration

You can connect slack to be alerted to configuration changes within your featureflow organisation.

Log in to your featureflow organisation as an Administrator and navigate to the admin > integrations tab.

Click the 'Add to slack button' and follow the prompts to select the channel you wish to be alerted in.

Any problems? Ping us on intercom or email support@featureflow.io
